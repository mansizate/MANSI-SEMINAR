INDIA

India is the seventh largest country in the world, with a total area of 3,287,263 sq km (1.26 million sq mi). In 1947, India gained independence from Great Britain after more than 200 years of colonization by the British Empire.

india is a country in Asia continent. India’s capital is New Delhi. India’s economy is expected to become third largest by 2050 (next only to China and USA). The currency of India is the rupee (INR). There are many different religions practiced throughout India. India is the world’s largest democracy.
